Pundamilia Crossing Odyssey

8 Days

#Pundamilia Crossing Odyssey


Overview

The country boast of prolific game-viewing locations not just in Africa, but in the world. Witness some of nature’s greatest spectacles while on safari in Kenya’s celebrated national parks. The 8 days Kenya safari is what we consider to be an ideal safari itinerary to see the best that Kenya has to offer. Take in some of Kenya’s top safari destinations; the spectacular Amboseli, Lake Naivasha, bird watchers paradise of Lake Nakuru, and the world-famous Masai Mara.

Day 1

Nairobi - Amboseli

The driver guide will pick you up in the morning and after briefings, depart immediately for Amboseli National Park. Arrive at around midday and check in at the lodge at lunch time. After lunch and a short rest, you will embark on a late afternoon game drive watching various wildlife in their natural habitat. Back to the lodge in the evening. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

Day 2

Amboseli

Wake up to amazing views of Mt Kilimanjaro weather permitting. After breakfast, carry packed lunch and engage in full day game drives in this wonderful park. You will have the opportunity of seeing big heard of elephants up close. Another exciting place will be the observation point where you will have a birds view of the expansive park below teaming with wildlife. There will also be an optional Maasai village visit. Back to the lodge in the evening for the dinner and overnight.

Day 3

Amboseli

Depart Amboseli after an early breakfast and drive to Lake Naivasha. On the way, you will have a brief stop at the Great Rift Valley view point. You will arrive at lunch time. Have lunch and a short rest then you will be driven to Hells Gate National Park where you will have a guided game drive safari among the wildlife. You can also hike in the park or cycle in the park at an extra cost. You will also enjoy a boat ride in the lake and have an opportunity to spot many different species of birds. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

Destination of Visit:

Lake Naivasha

Day 5

Lake Nakuru National Park

Carry the packed food and spend the whole day in this amazing park famous for the pink lesser and greater flamingos and abundant wildlife. You will enjoy a wide ecological diversity and varied habitats that range from Lake Nakuru itself to the surrounding escarpment and picturesque ridges. Enjoy the view of the Rothchild giraffes and indeed the most endangered giraffes. Other animals present are lions, Buffalos, Hyenas, Rhinoceros, Zebras and many others. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

Destination of Visit:

Lake Nakuru National Park

Day 7

Masai mara

After breakfast you leave the lodge carrying packed lunches and embark on a full day game viewing in Africa’s finest park. You will drive to various places in the expansive Masai Mara National Reserve. Experience watching many incredible animals in the park and also visiting the river where the migration usually happen. Watch the predators in the river like crocodiles and lions and have a fully guided tips on how they usually hunt and get their mills. You will also have a chance to see the other big cats like the cheetahs, Leopards, etc. other animals present are the Elephants, Buffalos, Topi, Wildebeest and many others. In the late afternoon, you will have an option of visiting a Masai cultural village as you head back to the lodge in the evening for the overnight.
