Pundamilia Expedition

#Pundamilia Expedition


Embark on an extraordinary 8-day journey with our Simba Pride Adventure package. This immersive safari experience offers a unique opportunity to witness the diverse wildlife and breathtaking landscapes of Kenya. Starting with a warm welcome at the airport, you'll be guided through the iconic Maasai Mara National Reserve for three exhilarating days. From the sweeping savannahs to the rugged terrain, every moment is a chance to witness the untamed beauty of nature.

Next, we venture to the Amboseli National Park for two days, known for its majestic views of Mount Kilimanjaro and its vast elephant herds. Then, a day at the stunning Lake Nakuru National Park awaits, where a boat ride and encounters with vibrant flamingos are just a few highlights. Finally, Nairobi National Park provides a perfect conclusion to your adventure, offering a diverse array of wildlife within the city's backdrop.

Our expert guides, including Maasai warriors, will lead you through each destination, providing in-depth knowledge and ensuring your safety. To make your experience truly tailor-made, choose from optional activities like a hot air balloon safari over Maasai Mara at sunrise and a guided tour by Maasai warriors. Additionally, enjoy recreational activities such as a boat ride on Lake Nakuru and a refreshing swim at the hot springs.

With comfortable accommodations, delicious meals, and seamless transportation, this package offers an unforgettable adventure for nature enthusiasts, wildlife lovers, and anyone seeking a true taste of Africa's untamed beauty.

Arrival in Masai Mara

Day 1

Your adventure begins as you arrive at the airport in Nairobi. Our friendly team will be there to greet you and provide a comfortable transfer to Maasai Mara. After settling into your accommodation, embark on an evening game drive to witness the mesmerizing wildlife as the sun sets over the savannah.
