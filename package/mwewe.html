#Mwewe Majesty Safari


Overview

A 5-day safari to Lake Elementaita, Lake Bogoria, and Maasai Mara promises an unforgettable adventure in some of Kenya's most stunning and wildlife-rich regions. This safari introduces you to the diverse landscapes, captivating wildlife, and vibrant cultures of East Africa.

Day 1

Departure to Lake Elementaita

Your safari begins with your arrival in Nairobi, the capital city of Kenya. Upon arrival at Jomo Kenyatta International airport, you will be warmly met by our driver guide. Depending on your arrival time, you may have the opportunity to explore some of Nairobi's attractions, such as the David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust or the Giraffe Centre. After a brief introduction depart to Lake Elementaita. Arrive in time for lunch at your hotel. Check in and enjoy lunch. Thereafter depart for an evening game drive in Soysambu conservancy with spectacular viewing of scenery, birdlife, wildlife and the stunning Lake Elmentaita. Dinner and overnight at your hotel

Day 2

Day trip to Lake Bogoria

Approximately 2-3 hours drive. Approximately 115 kms. On this day, you'll journey to Lake Bogoria, another fascinating Rift Valley lake. Lake Bogoria is famous for its hot springs and geysers, which attract flocks of flamingos and other bird species. You can take a walk along the shores to witness the unique geological features and enjoy a picnic lunch. Return to your hotel in Lake Elementaita. Dinner and overnight at your hotel.

Day 3

Masai Mara National Reserve

Approximately 5 Hours drive drive. Approximately 246 Kms After breakfast, you'll make your way to the renowned Maasai Mara National Reserve, one of Africa's premier wildlife destinations. The Maasai Mara is home to the Big Five (lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and rhinoceros) and hosts the Great Wildebeest Migration (seasonal). Arrive at your hotel in time for lunch. Check in and enjoy sumptuous lunch. After a brief afternoon rest, you'll embark on an afternoon/evening game drives to witness the incredible wildlife diversity, including lions, cheetahs, zebras, giraffes, and more. Return back to your hotel. Dinner and overnight at your hotel

Dinner and overnight at your hotel.

Day 5

Masai Mara to Nairobi

Approximately 5 to 6 Hours. Approximately 260 Kms.

After breakfast, you'll depart to Nairobi, where your safari will come to an end. Depending on your flight timings, you have the option of visiting Giraffe centre and later have lunch at Carnivore restaurant. Thereafter you will be dropped off at Jomo Kenyatta International airport for your flight back home or place of residence within Nairobi with lots of memories to cherish.
